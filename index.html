Welcome to SafeToGo

SafeToGo is your way of seeing how crowded any store, restaurant or public space is. Our goal is to help you feel safe amongst other people while minimizing the spread of the corona virus.

SKIP

Turn on your location

Before you begin make sure you have enabled Location Services either when it pops up or in your settings.

Settings > Privacy > Location > SafeToGo > Allow location access

SKIP

Find a specific public place

When you first enter you will see a overview of all the public places near your current location. Simply click on your chosen place and see the crowdedness status of this location.

SKIP

Save a location for later

If you want to save a location for later, just click on the favourite button. This way you will have easy access to all you favorite locations.
